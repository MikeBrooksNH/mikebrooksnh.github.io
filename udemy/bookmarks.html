TARGET_HOST=[hostname or IP]
# Remove the old key(s) from known_hosts
ssh-keygen -R $TARGET_HOST

# Add the new key(s) to known_hosts (and also hash the hostname/address)
ssh-keyscan -H $TARGET_HOST >> ~/.ssh/known_hosts

The next time you connect, you'll connect **without** being asked `Are you sure you want to continue connecting (yes/no)?`
since the keys will already be in the `known_hosts` file
